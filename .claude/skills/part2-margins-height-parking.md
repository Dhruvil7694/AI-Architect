## Permissible Uses in Margin (Reg 6.7.3)

**For DW1-2-3 (except row house, cottage industry, pre-schools):**
- Ancillary structures (parking garage, servant quarter, WC, bathroom): any zone, side/rear margins only, max 16.5 sqm ground coverage, max 3.0m height
- Surface water tank: up to 1.5m height
- Open/cantilever staircase: max 1.0m width, landing max 2.0m — side/rear only (not road side)
- Underground water tank, percolation well, bore well + pump room (max 2m×2m, height 2m)
- Partition wall: max 1.5m height along common boundary
- Weather sheds/projections: up to 0.60m; above 2.4m from ground; not habitable

**For all uses (non-industrial, non-institutional):**
- Security cabin (pre-fab only): max 2m×4m, max 3.0m height — in road side margin

**For Industrial and Institutional:**
- Security cabin: max 16.5 sqm, max 3.0m height — in road side margin; min 4.5m from building

**Other permitted margin uses:**
- Pedestrian access paths, ramps
- Common plot
- Vehicular ramp to basement: in side/rear margins (BU ≤2000 sqm AND height ≤25m)
- Electrical substation, transformer, box-type transformer, feeder pillar, meter room (per electric company norms) — side/rear only
- Refuge area (cantilever, for BH >25m): per Fire Safety Regulations 2016
- Fire escape staircase (for BH >45m): side/rear only
- Parking: in margins except access path from gate to staircase; if contiguous with hollow plinth parking (min 6.0m dimension)
- Basement: within side/rear margins after leaving 3.0m from boundary
- Porch: min 4.5m height; 4.5m clear space kept for fire vehicles
- Sky walk: not below 10.50m height

**Special Buildings in road side margin:**
- Porch (cantilever): up to 6m from building line, min 3.0m height — not counted in FSI
- Parking: allowed after leaving min 6.0m from boundary (6.0m used for shade trees)

## Basement (Reg 6.8)

### Margins for Basement:
- No basement in road side margin or gamtal/CW setback
- Side & rear basement margin: 3.0m from building unit boundary
- Combined shared basement parking: full marginal space (except road side) with adjacent owner's consent
- For BU <500 sqm: margins as per Reg 6.7.3; min 3.0m from common wall of adjacent existing building
- Basement permitted under common plot/internal road for parking only

### Extent:
- Basement for parking: BU ≥250 sqm
- Other uses: any size, one level only (multi-level only as per parking requirement)

### Height: Min 2.80m, Max 4.50m (clear height, beam bottom to finished floor)

### Permitted Uses in Basement:
- Parking, safe deposit vault, AC plant, grey water treatment, sewage treatment, water tank, storage (non-inflammable)
- NO habitable use
- Hospital basement: radiation device/therapy room, MRI/X-ray room
- Parking-only basement: other uses permitted at level-1 only, max 25% of total basement built-up area (counts towards FSI)

### Gamtal/CW Basement:
- No basement in required setback
- One level for parking if BU >300 sqm; two levels if BU >1500 sqm
- No water/drainage connection in basement

## Parking Requirements (Reg 6.15, Table 6.44)

| Use | Minimum Parking | Visitor's Parking |
|-----|----------------|-------------------|
| DW1, DW2 | 1 car per unit for >100–300 sqm plinth area; +1 car per 100 sqm thereafter | Nil |
| DW3 | 20% of Total Utilized FSI | 10% |
| Mixed Use / Mercantile / Religious / Hospitality / Transport | DW as applicable; Others: 30% FSI (BU ≤750 sqm), 40% (BU 750–2000), 50% (BU >2000) | Residential 10%; Commercial 20% |
| Assembly 1,2,3 | 50% of Total Utilized FSI | 20% |
| Assembly 4 | 50% of Building Unit Area | 20% |
| Assembly-Stadium | 1.25 sqm per person of total capacity | — |
| Institutional, Public-Institutional | 50% of Total Utilized FSI + ambulance at ground level for hospitals | 20% |
| Industrial 1-4, Storage | 10% of Total Utilized FSI | Nil |
| Educational: Primary/Pre-school | 25% of Total Utilized FSI | 10% |
| Educational: Secondary/Higher Secondary, Colleges | 40% of Total Utilized FSI + drop-off/pickup facility | 10% |
| Sports & Leisure | 25% of Building Unit Area | 10% |
| Recreation | 10% of Building Unit Area | 10% |

**Key parking rules:**
- 50% of visitor parking at ground level
- 50% of all required parking for cars
- Parking area includes space + driveway + aisles; excludes approach road, vehicular lift, ramps
- Parking in side/rear margins (not access road)
- Parking in road side margin: after 4.5m clear margin from boundary (BU >750 sqm) or all road side margin (BU ≤750 sqm)
- Multi-level parking requires vehicular ramp (or vehicular elevator with floor sprinklers)
- No parking within Atrium
- Misuse of parking = authority discontinues entire building use
- Stacked/mechanized parking: 1.5× credit (500 sqm counted as 750 sqm)
- Heritage structures (hospitality/sympathetic adaptive reuse): parking not required

**SPD4 (Transit Oriented Zone) — M1 use:** 35% of utilized FSI + 20% visitor parking.

## Open-to-Sky Space / OTS (Reg 6.16)

### Building Dimensions Rule:
- Building depth ≥9.0m from front open space: OTS of 5.6 sqm, no side <1.8m, at plinth level, for every 9m depth
- If building width ≤4.5m: OTS of 3.0 sqm, no side <1.5m
- Exception: if depth ≤18m AND openings on at least 2 sides over street or OTS ≥2.4m wide → no OTS required

### Minimum OTS Dimension (Table 6.45):
| Building Height | Gamtal & CWC | Other than Gamtal |
|----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| ≤10m | 1.20m | 1.5m |
| >10–≤16.5m | 2.0m | 1.5m |
| >16.5–≤25m | 3.0m | 2.5m |
| >25–≤45m | NIL | 3.0m |
| >45m | NIL | 6.0m |

Additional OTS beyond minimum counts towards FSI computation.

## Common Plot (Reg 6.17.5, Table 6.49)

| Use | Building Unit Area | Minimum Common Plot |
|-----|-------------------|---------------------|
| General (not industrial/institutional) | ≥2000 sqm | 10% of BU area (8% for CWC/Gamtal) |
| Industrial | ≤5000 sqm | Nil |
| Industrial | >5000–<20000 sqm | 8% of BU area |
| Industrial | ≥20000 sqm | 1600 sqm + 5% of area exceeding 20000 sqm |
| REG, EDU1-2, INST, Assembly, PI | ≥2000 sqm | 20% of building area |

**For BU <2000 sqm with building height >25m:** Common plot = 10% of BU or 200 sqm, whichever is more.

**Common plot rules:**
- Sub-divided such that each piece ≥200 sqm, no side <10m (RAH: no side <8m)
- Angle between adjacent sides ≥60°
- No construction except: electric substation, transformer, water tank, pump room, security cabin, community/society common amenities (max 15% ground coverage, max 7.5m height, community facility ≤50 sqm doesn't count to FSI)
- Residential use: 50% of common plot may be used for parking
- Non-residential use: 100% of common plot may be parking
- Parking in road side margin after min 1.5m from BU boundary (for shade trees)
- Vested in residents'/occupants' society on sanction of DP; cannot be sold

## Row Houses (Reg 6.13)
- Minimum plot area for row house complex: 1000 sqm
- Minimum common wall length: 50% of depth of row house
- Minimum individual sub-plot: 40 sqm; max built-up 60%; min width 3.0m
- Front margin: 2.0m; rear: 2.5m; end plot side: 2.0m (road side)
- 5.0m open space after every 10 continuous houses
- Maximum height: G+2, max 10.0m (from top of hollow plinth if parking at ground)

---

## Podium (Reg 6.14)
- Allowed for plots >4000 sqm
- Not in front setback/margin
- Min 6.0m from plot boundary all sides
- Structural design must bear fire engine loads
- Chief Fire Officer consent required
- Common plot may be at elevated level up to max 10m (below: swimming pool, banquet hall, parking); parapet ≥2.0m
- Podium for parking only
