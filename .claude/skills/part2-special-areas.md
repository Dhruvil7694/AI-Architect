# CGDCR-2017 Part II: Special Areas, Heritage, RAH, TOZ, Fuelling Stations

## Chapter 7: Gamtal (GM) & Core Walled City (CW)

### 7.1 Minimum Building Unit Area
18 sqm; no side <3.0m.

### 7.2 Setback (Gamtal/CW)
- 3.0m from centerline of existing street where no regular street line is prescribed
- Land left open = deemed part of street
- Authority may permit FSI on original area if owner releases land for road widening without compensation
- No setback on dead-end streets and service lanes
- If prescribed line <6.0m, first floor onwards may cover from 3.6m clear height

### 7.3 Building Height (Table 7.1)
| Road Width | Max Height |
|-----------|-----------|
| ≤7.5m | Twice road width or open space width |
| >7.5m–<12m | 16.5m |
| ≥12m | 25.0m |
Note: D3/D6 max = 11m. For khadki/chowk/alley/cul-de-sac: height regulated by road leading to it.

### 7.4 Ground Coverage (Table 7.2) — Core Walled City Ahmedabad
| Building Unit Area | Ground Coverage |
|-------------------|----------------|
| ≤500 sqm | 100% |
| >500–≤1500 sqm | 75% |
| >1500 sqm | 65% |
Gamtal: max built-up area on any floor including GF ≤75% of BU area.

### 7.5 Chowk for Gamtal
Building >9m depth from front must have open chowk ≥5.6 sqm (min side 1.5m) for every 9m depth. If width ≤4.5m: 3.0 sqm minimum. Exception: if openings on 2 sides over ≥2.4m wide space and depth ≤18m.

### 7.6 Heritage Conservation — Ahmedabad
- Heritage structures notified under Heritage Areas; remainder = "Other Areas of Core Walled City"
- Owners must conserve: façades, building footprint, character of open spaces (courtyards, khadki, streets)

**TDR for Heritage Areas:**
- Highest Heritage Value: 50% of utilized FSI as tradable floor space
- High/Moderate Heritage Value: 30% of utilized FSI
- Non-listed: Nil
- Max 10% of this TDR for extensions/alterations within max permissible FSI; rest via TDR mechanism in chargeable FSI zones

### 7.7 Heritage Core Area — Udawada Nagarpalika
**Heritage Conservation Committee (HCC) composition:** Prant Officer Valsad (Chairman), Sarpanch, Town Planner Valsad (Class I), Representative of Udavada Samast Anjuman, Architect-Heritage Expert (appointed by Udavada Samast Anjuman), Junior Town Planner Valsad (Member Secretary).

**Key rules:**
- No amalgamation/sub-division of plots in HCA
- No demolition/reconstruction/repair without HCC permission
- Change of use only with HCC approval
- No road widening in HCA; Iranshah road = pedestrian only
- All common/individual open spaces maintained without building
- FSI = net FSI of existing building (cannot exceed)
- No basements in any development/redevelopment
- Street edge of buildings must be maintained
- No compound wall in front where none existed originally; existing can be rebuilt transparent, max 1.1m above road centerline
- Staircases maintained in original form; no new additional staircases
- No stilts
- Max height: 9.0m
- Plinth: 0.6m–1.2m from ground
- Floor height: 2.7m–3.0m
- HCC to give opinion within 30 days of receiving proposal

## Chapter 8: Other than Gamtal & Core Walled City

### 8.1 Gamtal Extension (GME)
Extent: 300m from gamtal boundary (population <5000); 600m for others (unless demarcated in DP).

**Building Height (Table 8.1):** Same as Table 7.1 (≤7.5m: 2× road width; >7.5–<12m: 16.5m; ≥12m: 25m).

---

### 8.2 Residential Affordable Housing (RAH)

**Definition:** Residential development providing dwelling units up to 80 sqm with ancillary commercial up to 10% of total utilized FSI.

**Applicability:** All areas and zones EXCEPT:
- CW (Ahmedabad), Heritage Core (Udawada), Gamtal, R3
- All industrial zones (IZ1-7, GIDC IZ3-4)
- Agriculture zones (AGZ1-4, REZ)
- SPD2, SPD3

**Also applies to:** Mukhya Mantri Awas Yojana, Mukhya Mantri Gruh Yojana.

**FSI for RAH:**
- Minimum FSI to utilize: 1.8; Maximum: 2.7
- Base FSI as per respective zone; additional chargeable as per Table 8.2:

| Dwelling Unit Built-up Area | Additional FSI Charge |
|----------------------------|----------------------|
| ≤50 sqm | 10% of jantri rate |
| >50–≤66 sqm | 20% of jantri rate |
| >66–≤80 sqm | 30% of jantri rate |

- Staircase, passages, corridors also exempted from FSI computation (in addition to standard exemptions)

**Common Plot for RAH:** 10%

**Parking for RAH (Table 8.3):**
| Use | Min Parking | Visitor Parking |
|-----|-------------|----------------|
| Apartments ≤66 sqm | 10% of utilized FSI | +10% |
| Apartments >66 sqm | 20% of utilized FSI | 10% |
| Commercial | 50% of respective utilized FSI | 20% |

---

### 8.3 Transit Oriented Zone / Smart City Node (SPD4)

**Applicability:** High density development in any zone except industrial zones. MRT corridors applicable only after state govt notification of corridors.

**Local Area Plan:** Competent authority must prepare LAP and get government approval. LAP regulations prevail over CGDCR.

**Parking for M1 (Commercial) use (Table 8.4):** 35% of Total Utilized FSI + 20% visitor parking.

**Road widening relief:** Competent authority may relax only road side margin and parking for building units affected by road widening.

**Revenue from chargeable FSI:** Distributed between ULB, Development Authority, MRT, SSNNL etc. as decided by Competent Authority.

---

### 8.4 Special Areas

#### 8.4.1 Closed Textile Mills Zone (CZ)
- Permissible uses: KZ (Knowledge Zone) and RAH uses
- FSI (Table 8.5): Base 1.8; Chargeable 2.2 (within TOZ), 0.9 (outside TOZ); at 40% jantri rate
- DW1-2-3 permitted only with GPCB NOC
- 40% land contribution to competent authority for development other than industrial use

#### 8.4.2 Gandhi Ashram (GACAIS) — AUDA
- Buildings owned by Gandhi Ashram Trusts (TRGA): conserved/retained in original state
- Abutting Ashram Road (Wadaj to Subhash Circle, owned by Govt/TRGA/AMC): residential + institutional + tourist facilities + hotel/restaurant/showroom for art/handcraft
- Max permissible height: 10m
- Exterior: white/light shades only; match Gandhi Ashram ambiance
- Trees planted as prescribed by authority

#### 8.4.3 Knowledge Precinct, CBD (C5) & Science Park
- Local Area Plans required including street dev, parking management, pedestrianization
- Science Park (SPD3): R1 uses and planning regulations apply

#### 8.4.4 Sabarmati Riverfront (SPD2)
SRFDCL special regulations on ground coverage, margins, height, use prevail.

---

### 8.5 Restricted Zones

**RZ1:** Structures necessary to control floods/prevent river/pond/lake pollution.

**RZ2:** Flood control structures + development ≤5% of BU (ground floor only) to preserve/conserve natural scenery.

**RZ3:** No development permitted.

**RZ4:** Structures to preserve historical/natural/architectural/scientific interest (around Laxmi Vilas Palace area).

**Restricted Residential Zone — VUDA:** 50% of plot per R1 regulations; remaining 50% only RAH regulations (max unit 80 sqm; 50% of those units max 40 sqm).

**Restricted Residential Zone — JADA:** R1 uses, height restriction ≤10m; NOC from defence authority required.

**Hazard Prone Area:** Any development subject to NOC and permission of Competent Authority.

**Defence Zone:** As per defence and Central Government authorities' regulations.

**Vacant Land:** No development activity.

## Chapter 9: Mining, Quarrying, Brick Kiln

- Without blasting: permitted within 75m of public road, railway, canal, transmission line, other building
- With blasting: NOT permitted within 200m of any public road, railway, canal, transmission line, building
- No building operations on same plot without prior authority approval
- Permission for stipulated period not exceeding 3 years at a time
- Below average ground level: permitted only for extraction
- Brick kiln: minimum 25% fly ash use mandatory

## Chapter 10: Fuelling & Electric Vehicle (eV) Charging Stations

### 10.1 Minimum Area and Frontage (Table 10.1)
| Type | Min Area | Min Frontage |
|------|----------|--------------|
| Fuelling/eV Station (without service) | 500 sqm | 16.5m |
| Fuelling/eV with Service Station | 2000 sqm (D1-D7A); 500 sqm (D7B/D8/D10) | 30m |

### 10.2 Location
- Only on roads ≥18m wide, in any zone
- Min 50m from junction where any road is >18m wide
- Min 30m from other junctions
- If service road/marginal access road exists: access from service road only, not main road

### 10.3 Ground Coverage
Max 45% of building unit including canopy area.

### 10.4 Margins
**Without Service Station:**
- Road side (canopy to boundary): per Reg 6.7, minimum 6.0m
- Other sides (canopy to boundary): 4.5m (canopy ≤6m height); 6.0m (canopy >6m)
- Fuelling pedestal to main road boundary: min 9.0m; other sides: 6.0m
- Admin building: min 3.0m side/rear margins
- Min 6.0m clear driveway between every pedestal
- Min access road width: 6.0m

**With Service Station:**
- All margins: 6.0m (road side); min 6.0m road side, 3.0m other sides for service station building
- 6.0m clear between pedestal and service station

### 10.5 Height, FSI, Common Plot
- Max height: 7.5m
- FSI: as per relevant zone
- Common plot: not required

### 10.6 Parking (Table 10.2)
| BU Area | Min Parking |
|---------|------------|
| 500 sqm | 3 cars + 5 two-wheelers |
| 2000 sqm (D7B/D8: 500 sqm) | +1 car + 2 two-wheelers per 500 sqm above 1000 sqm |

### 10.7 Basement
No basement allowed.

## Chapter 11: Advertising Display & Communication Infrastructure

### 11.1 Billboards/Hoardings in Marginal Space (Table 11.1)
- 1 billboard per BU (≤500 sqm); max 2 per BU (>500 sqm)

| Location | Road Width | Max Size (W×H) | Min Road Side Margin | Min Ground Clearance | Max Height |
|----------|-----------|----------------|---------------------|---------------------|-----------|
| Marginal open space | ≤60m | 9.0×4.5m | 0.6m | 3.0m | 16.5m |
| Marginal open space | >60m | 12.0×6.0m | 1.5m | 3.0m | 16.5m |

### 11.1.2 Billboards on Rooftops/Terrace (Table 11.2)
| Road Width | Max Size | Max No. | Side Margin | Between Boards | Max Height from Terrace |
|-----------|----------|---------|-------------|----------------|------------------------|
| ≤60m | 12.0×6.0m | 2 (different facing directions) | 1.5m from parapet | 1.5m | 7.5m |
| >60m | 18.0×9.0m | — | — | — | 10.5m |

Note: Max height is over and above permissible/existing building height. Billboard must not extend beyond building line.

### 11.1.3 Billboards on Public Roads (Table 11.3)
| Road Width | Min Ground Clearance | Max Size (W×H) |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------|
| ≤60m | 3.0m | 9.0×4.5m |
| >60m | 3.0m | 12.0×6.0m |

### 11.1.6 General Restrictions
Billboards PROHIBITED if they:
- Obstruct vision for safe traffic movement
- Could be confused with traffic signs/signals
- Contain words like "Stop", "Look", "Danger"
- Are prohibited under local/state/national law
- Overhang public property obstructing access/egress
- Contain unfair/vulgar/objectionable content
- Project outside building unit boundary

### 11.2 Communication Infrastructure (Towers)
Applicable to: post/telegraph, telephone, wireless, broadcasting, cellular, internet, V-SAT, ATM, channel discs, overhead lines.

**Location:** On building rooftop/terrace ≥1.5m from parapet, OR on ground/open space within premises.
