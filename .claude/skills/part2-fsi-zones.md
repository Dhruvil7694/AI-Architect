**Notes:**
- Commercial use on G+1 floor permitted on road ≤12 mts if 7.5m setback from center line is kept (land handed over to authority, FSI based on original area)
- Industrial uses and PU are not regulated by road width
- Non-residential uses in residential zones may be permitted on any floor if separate staircase/access provided
- Professional use (doctor, lawyer, architect, CA, engineer) up to max 50 sq.mt + commercial parking

## Areas Not Counted in FSI (Reg 6.3.2)

1. Interior open-to-sky spaces (courtyards, chowks, utility ducts)
2. Parking at basement, hollow plinth, or any level
3. Basement exclusively for parking (min clear height 2.8m, max 4.5m); other uses up to 25% of basement area count towards FSI
4. Hollow plinth for parking (max clear height 3.5m); allowed ancillary: meter room, telephone board, common toilet, water room, servant room, security cabin, entrance foyer — total ≤16.5 sqm
5. Loft up to 30% of enclosing space
6. Staircase, sky walks, intermediate landing, stair cabin (width = 2x stair width + 0.5x each side)
7. Lift well, lift cabin, stair cabin, lift landing (width = lift well width + 0.5x each side; depth = 2x lift well width)
8. Vehicular and pedestrian ramps
9. Electric room/substation as specified by Competent Electric Company
10. Open-to-sky space for solar heating, AC outdoor units, mechanical ventilation
11. Common amenities in common plot up to 50 sqm
12. Pergolas
13. Skip floor, refuge area (as per fire regulations)

## FSI Relaxation (Reg 6.3.3)
For building units affected by road widening or new road: FSI may be calculated on original boundary. Owner must surrender affected land before development permission.
