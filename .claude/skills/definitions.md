# CGDCR-2017 Definitions (Part I, Chapter 2)

## Key Terms

**Act** — Gujarat Town Planning & Urban Development Act 1976, or Gujarat Municipalities Act 1963, or GIDC Act 1962, or GDA Act 1957, as applicable.

**Additions/Alterations** — Any change in existing authorised building, change of use, structural change (area/height), removal of part, change to walls/beams/floors, or change to means of ingress/egress.

**Apartment/Flats** — Multi-storied residential buildings (detached or semi-detached), ground floor + upper floors, separate dwelling units with common staircase.

**Atrium** — Unobstructed multi-storied open space within a building covered from top with lightweight/glazed roof.

**Balcony** — Horizontal projection (cantilevered or otherwise), including parapet/handrail, to serve as passage or sit-out.

**Basement/Cellar** — Lower storey with at least half of clear floor height below average ground level.

**Building Types:**
- **Dwelling-1 (DW1)**: Detached dwelling unit for residential purpose
- **Dwelling-2 (DW2)**: Semi-detached, row house, tenement, farm house, cottage industry, pre-school
- **Dwelling-3 (DW3)**: Multi-storey (apartments, flats, hostels, lodging/boarding, pre-school)
- **Special Building**: Theatre, cinema, assembly hall, auditorium, town hall, exhibition hall, stadium, community hall, marriage hall
- **Hazardous Building**: Storage/handling of radioactive, combustible, explosive, corrosive, toxic materials
- **Unsafe Building**: Structurally unsafe, insanitary, inadequate egress, fire hazard, dangerous to life

**Building Height Categories:**
- H1: ≤ 16.50 mts
- H2: > 16.50 and ≤ 30 mts
- H3: > 30.0 and ≤ 45.0 mts
- H4: > 45.0 mts

**Building Line** — Control line up to which plinth of building may lawfully extend (as per TP Scheme/Development Plan).

**Building Unit** — A land/plot or combination of plots as approved by Competent Authority, excluding area falling in road alignment.

**Built-up Area** — Area covered by building on all floors including cantilevered portions and walls/columns, excluding areas specifically exempted.

**Chowk** — Fully or partially unclosed space permanently open to sky within a building at any level.

**Common Plot** — Common open space exclusive of approaches and margin, at ground level or higher levels (not exceeding 10 mts from ground level), vested in residents' society on sanction of development permission.

**Competent Authority** — Person/authority authorized by Appropriate Authority or Municipal Commissioner.

**Developer** — Person legally empowered to construct/execute work on building-unit; or owner where no such person is empowered.

**Dwelling Unit** — Self-contained unit of accommodation providing living, cooking, and sanitary facilities used by one household.

**Farm House** — Building used for agricultural/farm activity in agricultural zone, incidental to agricultural use.

**Floor Space Index (FSI)** — Ratio of total floor area of all floors to the area of the building unit. FSI = Total floor area / Building unit area.

**Gamtal** — Original settlement of a village; boundaries as demarcated by Revenue Department.

**Hollow Plinth** — Open ground floor area on columns/pillars used exclusively for parking/circulation (maximum clear height 3.5 mts).

**Loft** — Intermediate floor between two main floors, with maximum 30% of enclosing space.

**Margin (Setback)** — Open space required to be left between building and plot boundary.

**Open-to-Sky Space (OTS)** — Space open to sky, required for light and ventilation.

**Parapet** — Low wall built along edge of roof/terrace/balcony.

**Pergola** — Open structure of columns supporting a roofing grid of beams/rafters with open gaps, not enclosed.

**Plinth** — The portion of a structure between surface of surrounding ground and surface of floor immediately above the ground.

**Podium** — Continuous projecting base or pedestrian area under/around building, used for parking and vehicle movement.

**Public Purpose** — Includes: sanitary works, housing for public/workers, development of scheduled areas, drainage, transport, education, medical relief, public offices, parks, water supply, markets, etc.

**Refuge Area** — Area where persons unable to use stairways remain temporarily and await rescue during emergency.

**Road Width** — Whole extent of space within road boundaries (new road as per DP/TP); or clear average width of existing carriageway and footpaths.

**Row House** — Group of residential buildings on adjacent plots (with/without common walls), joined by common walls, with only front and rear open spaces.

**Service Apartment** — Furnished apartment with kitchen/cooking facility, available for short/long-term stays.

**Skip Floor** — Floor designated for holding occupants during fire emergencies; may be used as service areas, not habitable.

**TDR (Tradable Development Rights)** — Mechanism where tradable floor space assigned to one building can be traded for use on another building unit.

**Use Categories (Table 6.3 codes):**
- DW1: Detached dwelling, clinic (no indoor treatment)
- DW2: Semi-detached, row house, tenement, farm house, cottage industry
- DW3: Apartment, hostel, dharamshala, pre-school, affordable housing
- M1: Shop, restaurant, shopping centre, LPG godown, service establishment, clinic, fitness centre
- M2: Shopping mall, lab, nursing home, corporate office, call centre, training centre
- M3: Wholesale market, indoor hospital, diamond industry, IT activity
- EDU1: Primary/secondary/higher secondary schools
- EDU2: College, polytechnic, university
- AS1: Community hall, banquet hall, town hall
- AS2: Convention centre, exhibition hall, auditorium, planetarium, stadium, museum, wadi
- AS3: Theatre, cinema, multiplex, clubs, golf course
- AS4: Party plot, garden restaurants
- INST: Research centres, hospital (INST1: Dwellings/retail as part of educational institute up to 20% FSI)
- REG: Temples, church, mosque, gurudwara, any religious structure
- HO: Bed & breakfast, guest house, hotel, motel, serviced apartment
- SE: Auto repair, workshop, fabrication, public garage
- IN1: Light/service industries, small factories, warehouses, printing press, poultry farm, dairy
- IN1A: Tile/ceramic/clay/glass manufacturing industries
- IN2: All industries except obnoxious/hazardous, textile units, stone quarrying, steel re-rolling
- IN3: Slaughter house, meat/leather processing, cold storage, agro industries
- IN4: Fuel storage, thermal power plant, obnoxious/hazardous industries, chemical industries
- ST: Warehouse, godown, cold storage, timber mart
- SCK: Small agricultural storage (max 30m × 12m)
- TR: Truck/bus terminal, transport terminal
- AG1: Horticulture, agricultural equipment sale/repair, dairy, animal rearing, brick kiln, farm house
- AG2: Agricultural vocational training, mining, ship building, aquaculture
- AG3: Poultry farm, agro-based godowns
- TEMP: Fair, circus, exhibition, mela, pandal
- PU: Sub-station, bus station, fuelling/EV station, parking, water supply, fire station
- PI: Government/semi-govt offices, public facilities, post office, police chowki
- H1: PHC, clinic, dispensary
- H2: Indoor hospital up to 20 beds, surgical hospital, nursing home
- H3: Civil hospital, medical college, hospital > 20 beds
- H4: Hospital for infectious diseases, mental hospital
- TO: Tourism sponsored activity
- REC: Natural reserve, race track, zoo, gardens, botanical garden, play fields
- SP&L: Sports complex, swimming pool, theme park, aquarium, zoo
