# CGDCR-2017: Comprehensive General Development Control Regulations

Gujarat's unified building & development regulations issued by Urban Development and Urban Housing Department (UD & UHD), Govt. of Gujarat. Notified 12 Oct 2017 (GH/V/269), amended 15 Jan 2019 (GH/V/5).

## Critical Rules to Always Remember

1. **Road width drives everything** — uses, FSI, height, and margins all vary by abutting road width.
2. **Category drives FSI** — D1-AUDA, D1-GUDA-GMC, D1-GUDA-non-GMC, D1-SUDA, D1-VUDA, D1-RUDA, D2, D3-D10 all have different tables.
3. **Base + Chargeable = Maximum FSI** — base FSI is free; chargeable is paid at 40% of jantri rate (unless RAH or special zone).
4. **Fuelling Stations and Public-Institutional (PI/PU) are permitted in ANY zone.**
5. **Fire Act overrides CGDCR** where they conflict (Reg 1.1.6).
6. **Heritage areas**: Udawada HCA has its own Heritage Conservation Committee (HCC) with strict rules.
7. **Non-TP areas**: 40% land contribution required for D1/D2; 12m road must be provided elsewhere.

### Part III Performance Regulations — Key Rules

8. **Rain water harvesting** is mandatory for all buildings with ground coverage ≥80 sq.mt.
9. **Lift mandatory** for buildings >10 mts height; 1 lift per 30 DUs (residential) or per 1000 sq.mt BUA (non-residential). Fire lift mandatory >25 mts.
10. **Mixed development >45 mts not permitted.** Commercial+residential must be separated by skip/service floor (max 2.1 mts clear height).
11. **Disability provisions** (accessible entrance, parking, WC, lift) required for all buildings except Dwelling-1&2.
12. **Grey water recycling** mandatory for hospitals >5000 sq.mt, hotels/commercial/hostels >10,000 sq.mt.
13. **SEIAA Environmental Clearance** required before development permission for built-up area ≥20,000 sq.mt (buildings) or ≥50 Ha / 1,50,000 sq.mt (townships).
14. **NOC from Police Commissioner** required for Assembly, Religious, Hospitality, Party Plot, and Fuelling/EV station uses.
15. **Structural Inspection Report (SIR)** required for all Schedule 6 buildings; periodic SEOR inspection mandatory for Class 1 & 2 buildings.
16. **No direct road entry to basement** — access per Fire Prevention & Life Safety Reg-2016.
17. **Solar PV** mandatory for plotted housing >1000 sq.mt and commercial/institutional buildings on plots ≥1000 sq.mt with shadow-free roof >50 sq.mt.
